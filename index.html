SRA JOHN

A brief overview of some my experiences in the United States Air Force.

Basic Training

"It doesn't get better, you do"

Basic training taught me a lot of things. How to lead, how to follow and how to walk really fast past my instructors during lunch time. Out of everything I learned in basic training my major take away was this:

Much like the rest of life, things never get easier. You just get better at doing them.
